The Problem

Concert-goers in Vienna face challenges when trying to discover, check availability, and purchase tickets for live events in the city. Current solutions are fragmented and lack a streamlined, user-friendly experience, often requiring users to navigate multiple platforms and interfaces. As a result, users experience frustration from limited event discovery, cumbersome ticket purchasing processes, and inconsistent information across different sources.

The Goal

Develop ViennaELECTRO, a mobile application designed to help young people discover and attend electronic and techno music events in Vienna. Provides an intuitive interface where users can filter upcoming parties by time frame and ensure that users can easily find events that fit their schedules, enhancing their nightlife experience.

Key Objectives

  1. User-Friendly Interface - Intuitive and simple user interface that allows users to easily navigate through concert listings, event details, and ticket purchasing options.
  2. Comprehensive Event Discovery - Provide an extensive and up-to-date database of concerts happening around Vienna, including detailed event information such as date, time, venue, and genre.
  3. Real-Time Availability and Pricing - Ensure real-time ticket availability and transparent pricing for each event.
  4. Secure and Easy Ticket Purchase - Offer a seamless and secure payment system, with a smooth checkout process and clear confirmation.

Solution Statement

To address the challenges faced by concert-goers in Vienna, we will design a mobile app that serves as a comprehensive, user-friendly platform for discovering, checking availability, and purchasing concert tickets. The app will feature a streamlined interface that makes it easy for users to browse upcoming events, view real-time ticket availability, and make secure, hassle-free purchases.

02. Market Research

Existing Apps in the Market

There are several well-established apps and platforms catering to music events and ticketing, though not all focus exclusively on electronic and techno concerts. Some of the key players include: Songkick, RA (Resident Advisor), Bandsintown, Eventbrite

Trends in the Electronic Music Market

Techno & Electronic Popularity: Electronic music, especially techno, has been on the rise in many global markets, including Vienna. Venues like Prater Dome, Grelle Forelle, and SASS Music Club frequently host electronic and techno events, indicating a robust local demand.

Festival Culture: Many electronic and techno music fans attend festivals (e.g., Austrian festivals like Life Ball, Electric Love, or the more niche events), and apps that cater to the festival-going crowd (with lineups, maps, and ticketing integration) have a distinct advantage.

Opportunities and Gaps

Local Focus: While apps like RA cover events worldwide, a hyper-local focus on Vienna, with deep integration into the local electronic music scene, could set the app apart. Promoting lesser-known venues, underground events, and up-and-coming artists could appeal to fans seeking a more intimate experience.

Mobile-First Design: A mobile-first design with intuitive event browsing, map integration (to help users find venues), and user-friendly ticketing would ensure ease of use for fans on the go.

Potential Partnerships

Venue Partnerships: Partnering with popular venues in Vienna (such as SASS Music Club or Arena Wien) could help secure exclusive content, early-bird ticket offers, or VIP access.

Local DJs and Artists: Building partnerships with Viennaâ€™s well-known techno and electronic DJs and promoting their upcoming events could boost user engagement and ensure the app stays relevant to the local scene.

03. User Research

Target Audience

We could categorize the target audience into two main groups: primary and secondary users.

1. Primary Users

Demographics: Young adults (18-35), primarily tech-savvy individuals, passionate about electronic music and club culture.

Location: Users living in or near Vienna, but also tourists who may be visiting for electronic music festivals or events.

Music Preferences: Fans of electronic music genres like techno, house, trance, dubstep, and minimal, often seeking underground or mainstream events.

2. Secondary Users

Event Organizers & Venues: Clubs, promoters, and DJs who need to reach potential attendees and promote their events effectively.

User Interviews and other Research Methods

Surveys & Questionnaires

  1. Event Discovery: How do users typically discover electronic music events in Vienna (e.g., social media, word of mouth, event apps)?
  2. Ticket Purchasing: What platforms do users prefer for buying tickets (e.g., RA, Eventbrite, direct from venues)?
  3. Pain Points: What are the main frustrations with current concert apps (e.g., confusing UI, lack of event details, pricing issues)?
  4. Features & Preferences: What features would users want in a concert app (e.g., personalized recommendations, artist tracking, social sharing, last-minute tickets)?
  5. Frequency of Events: How often do users attend electronic music events? (e.g., weekly, monthly, festival season)

Interviews

  1. User Motivation: Why do you attend electronic music events? What do you enjoy about the experience (e.g., the music, the atmosphere, meeting new people)?
  2. Event-Going Habits: How do you typically plan for events? Do you prefer to buy tickets well in advance or last-minute? Do you follow specific artists or festivals?
  3. App Usage: What apps do you currently use for event discovery and ticket purchasing? What do you like or dislike about these apps?
  4. Expectations: What would an ideal electronic music event app look like for you? What would make you more likely to use it regularly?

04. Ideation

First Ideas, Sitemap and Features

I. Home Page

The main interface of the app showcases upcoming events, allowing users to access the filters quickly (today, this weekend, and next week). This page serves as the central hub for event discovery.

II. Event Details Page

Users can tap on any event to view detailed information, including the venue, lineup, event timing, and descriptions. This page also includes user ratings and reviews to help inform decisions.

III. Ticket Checkout Process

Once users decide to attend an event, they can seamlessly purchase tickets through the app. The checkout process is streamlined for ease of use, with multiple payment options available.

IV. Profile Page

Users can create and manage their profiles, where they can update personal information, track their favorite events, and manage settings. This personalization enhances user engagement.

V. Wishlist

This feature allows users to save events they are interested in attending for future reference. Lisa can easily keep track of parties she wants to check out later.

VI. Purchased Tickets for Upcoming Events

Users can view the purchased tickets in one place. This page serves as a digital wallet for event tickets, making it easy for users to access their tickets on the day of the event.

VII. Dark and Light Theme

The app includes customizable themes, allowing users to switch between a dark mode and a light mode. This feature enhances user comfort by accommodating different lighting conditions and personal preferences, ensuring a pleasant experience whether they're browsing events during the day or late at night.
