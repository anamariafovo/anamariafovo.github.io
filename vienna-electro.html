App Overview

Vienna ELECTRO is a mobile application designed to help young people discover and attend electronic and techno music events in Vienna. The app provides an intuitive interface where users can filter upcoming parties by time frame: today, this weekend, and next week. This ensures that users can easily find events that fit their schedules, enhancing their nightlife experience.

Design Process

  1. Research - identify the problem and research similar products
  2. Ideation - identify user persona and target market
  3. Build the Sitemap - a general idea of how the app will be structured
  4. User Flows
  5. Mockups and Prototyping - in Figma
  6. Iterate previous steps and refine the design until it is complete and tested

Case Study - User Persona Example

Name: Lisa Mueller

Age: 24

Occupation: Graphic Designer

Location: Vienna, Austria

Lifestyle: Lisa is an outgoing and creative individual who loves exploring the local nightlife. She enjoys attending music events, especially electronic and techno parties, as they provide an opportunity to socialize and express herself through dance. She often seeks out new experiences and enjoys discovering hidden gems in the city.

Goals: To find exciting electronic music events in Vienna and easily plan her weekend or spontaneous nights out.

Problems: Difficulty in finding reliable sources for event information, overwhelmed by the number of options and unreliable event listings, limited time to plan outings due to her busy work schedule.

App Features

Home Page: The main interface of the app showcases upcoming events, allowing users to access the filters quickly (today, this weekend, and next week). This page serves as the central hub for event discovery.

Event Details Page: Users can tap on any event to view detailed information, including the venue, lineup, event timing, and descriptions. This page also includes user ratings and reviews to help inform decisions.

Ticket Checkout Process: Once users decide to attend an event, they can seamlessly purchase tickets through the app. The checkout process is streamlined for ease of use, with multiple payment options available.

Profile Page: Users can create and manage their profiles, where they can update personal information, track their favorite events, and manage settings. This personalization enhances user engagement.

Wishlist Page: This feature allows users to save events they are interested in attending for future reference. Lisa can easily keep track of parties she wants to check out later.

Purchased Tickets Page: Users can view the purchased tickets in one place. This page serves as a digital wallet for event tickets, making it easy for users to access their tickets on the day of the event.

Dark and Light Theme Feature: The app includes customizable themes, allowing users to switch between a dark mode and a light mode. This feature enhances user comfort by accommodating different lighting conditions and personal preferences, ensuring a pleasant experience whether they're browsing events during the day or late at night.

Why Vienna ELECTRO is useful?

Curated Content: Vienna ELECTRO focuses exclusively on electronic and techno music events, filtering out irrelevant information. This specialization allows users like Lisa to quickly find events that match their interests.

User-Friendly Filters: The app’s main page features straightforward filters (today, this weekend, next week), making it simple for users to plan their outings based on availability. This is particularly useful for users who have busy schedules and want to maximize their social experiences without extensive searching.

Real-Time Updates: The app provides real-time updates on events, including changes in venue or lineup. This ensures that users are always informed and can adjust their plans accordingly.

Discovery of New Venues: Vienna ELECTRO can introduce users to lesser-known venues hosting electronic music events, enriching their nightlife experience and supporting local businesses.

In-app Ticketing: Users can purchase tickets directly through the app for events, simplifying the process and ensuring they don’t miss out on popular parties.

Conclusion

Vienna ELECTRO addresses the needs of young people in Vienna who are passionate about electronic music. By providing a streamlined platform for discovering and planning nightlife experiences, the app empowers users like Lisa to engage with their city's vibrant music scene effortlessly. With its focus on user experience and community connection, Vienna ELECTRO is positioned to become an essential tool for electronic music enthusiasts in the city.
